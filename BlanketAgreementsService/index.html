Blanket Agreements Service

Description

The BlanketAgreementsService service enables you to add, look up, cancel, and update blanket agreements.

To open the blanket agreement window, choose Business Partners -> Blanket Agreement.

To display the available blanket agreements, choose Business Partners -> Business Partner Reports -> Blanket Agreements List.
