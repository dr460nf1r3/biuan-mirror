Employee Transfers Service

Description

EmployeeTransfersService is a business object that manages employee master data transfers from SAP Business One to the DATEV HR client application.

Source table: OHET.

Remarks

To transfer employee information from SAP Business One:

  1. Choose Human Resources --> Human Resources Reports --> Employee List.
  2. Select the desired employee from the list.
  3. Choose the Transfer button.
