Dunning Term DunningTerm Object· Table: ODUT
Represents a dunning term, which defines a set of dunning levels that determine when to send dunning letters for past-due balances. A dunning term can be assigned to a business partner. Source table: ODUT Mandatory properties: Code, Name;    Parent: Dunning Terms Service DunningTermsService Service· Table: ODUT
